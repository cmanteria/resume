Like many of us, my life extends beyond discussing technology and running my business. I have a variety of personal interests that I'm passionate about. Here are a few of them.

Cooking

Cooking is my passion. It's how I show love to those in my home and take care of our daily need to eat. It’s my creative outlet, allowing me to constantly learn and grow skills that I adore. From everyday cooking and baking to specialized crafts like sausage making, indulging in sourdough obsessions, and mastering real barbeque and meat curing, I love dabbling in all of it—much to the delight of my friends and family!

Reading

Books have always been a cherished part of my life. Growing up, I often found myself lost in the pages of fantasy and science fiction novels, exploring distant lands and forgotten times. As I've gotten older, my literary tastes have expanded to include philosophy, government, religion, and personal growth. I've been fortunate enough to create a space that serves as both my library and office, where I get to work and indulge in my passion for reading every day.
