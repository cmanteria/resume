As with most of us I don't just live to talk technology and run business but have outside interests. Here are a few of mine.

Cooking

I love to cook. It's a passion. It's a way to show love for those in my home and a way to provide for our daily need to eat. It's a creative outlet and allows me to continue to learn and grow skills that I just adore. From daily cooking and baking to craft cooking such as sausage making, sourdough obsessions, and of course real barbeque and meat curing I love to dabble in all of it, much to the delight of my friends and family


Reading

Books have always been something that I love. More often then not I was delving into some long lost land or time forgotten with a fantasy or science fiction novel. As I have gotten older, I have continued this passion but find that I have added in texts on philosophy, government, religion, and personal growth. I've been lucky enough to carve out this space as both my library and office where I get to work every day.
